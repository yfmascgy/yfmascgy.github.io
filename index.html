Yunfei Ma

Postdoctoral Associate

Media Lab
Massachusetts Institute of Technology

Email:yunfeima@mit.edu
Address: E14-474 Amherst Street,
Cambridge, MA 02139

I am currently a postdoctoral associate in Signal Kinetics group at MIT Media Lab, supervised by Prof. Fadel Adib. My research explores and develops technologies and algorithms in wireless computer networks. I received Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Cornell University in 2016 and a B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2010. I have been a Senior Engineer at the Oracle (Sun Microsystems) in Santa Clara, CA in 2016 and Engineering Intern at Qualcomm (Advanced System Integration) in San Diego, CA in 2015.
