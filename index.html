Yunfei Ma

Researcher
Alibaba Group US

Email:yunfei.ma@alibaba-inc.com
Address: 525 Almanor Ave.,
Sunnyvale, CA 94085

I am currently a Researcher/Staff Engineer/Engineering Manager at Alibaba Group Sunnyvale. My current focus is to build the next generation mobile and wide area networking technologies for Alibaba's global network infrastructer that supports Alibaba core businesses including e-commerce (e.g., Taobao, Tmall, Ele.me), video conferencing (e.g., Dingtalk) and AliCloud. Before joining Alibaba, I was a postdoctoral associate in Signal Kinetics group at MIT Media Lab, supervised by Prof. Fadel Adib. My research explores and develops technologies and algorithms in wireless & mobile networks. I received Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Cornell University in 2016 and a B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2010, where I was in the 00-class program (理科实验班). I have been a Senior Engineer at the Oracle (Sun Microsystems) in Santa Clara, CA in 2016 and Engineering Intern at Qualcomm in San Diego, CA in 2015. I was born and raised in Xuzhou, Jiangsu Province, and I graduated from Xuzhou No.1 Middle School.
