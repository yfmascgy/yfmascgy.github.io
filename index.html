Researcher
Alibaba Group US

Email:yunfei.ma@alibaba-inc.com
Address: 525 Almanor Ave.,
Sunnyvale, CA 94085

I am currently a Staff Engineer/Researcher at Alibaba Group Sunnyvale. Before joining Alibaba, I was a postdoctoral associate in Signal Kinetics group at MIT Media Lab, supervised by Prof. Fadel Adib. My research explores and develops technologies and algorithms in wireless computer networks. I received Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Cornell University in 2016 and a B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2010, where I was in the 00-class program (理科实验班). I have been a Senior Engineer at the Oracle (Sun Microsystems) in Santa Clara, CA in 2016 and Engineering Intern at Qualcomm (Advanced System Integration) in San Diego, CA in 2015. I was born and raised in Xuzhou, Jiangsu Province, and I graduated from Xuzhou No.1 Middle School.
